About Me

I'm Matthew Selva, an undergraduate student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) and a part of the class of 2022. I'm currently pursuing a double major in Interactive Media & Game Development and Computer Science with an expected graduation in May 2022. Throughout my time at WPI, I have created a variety of projects both individually and in small groups. Many of these projects are listed below.

Slip N' Slide

JavaScript | Perlenspiel Engine

A block sliding puzzle created using Perlenspiel, a web-based JavaScript engine created by our professor, Brian Moriarty. The goal for each level is to slide the white square to the hole to progress. Two additional mechanics are introduced and some levels can be played non-linearly using a level selection screen.
